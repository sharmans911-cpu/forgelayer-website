About ForgeLayer

Home-based custom 3D printing studio — based in Germany

ForgeLayer is a small, passion-driven 3D printing business run from home. We specialise in custom, made-to-order prints — from practical home organisation items to personalised gifts and functional prototypes. Every item is printed fresh for you, in the colour and size you need.

Why ForgeLayer?

  • Every item is printed to order — no stock, no waste
  • Custom sizes, colours and text always available
  • You receive a photo before we ship
  • Payment only after you are happy with the result
  • Free quote, no obligation — just send us an email

Our setup

We print using a Bambu Lab A1 series printer with high-quality PLA and PETG filaments. Available in 6 colours: Black, White, Red, Pink, Orange, Sky Blue and Grey. PETG is used for heat-resistant and tougher items on request.

Based in Germany

We ship within Germany via DHL. Local pickup is also available. Most small items are ready within 2–4 working days.
